Frequently Asked Questions

What taper haircut suits my face shape?

Our AI analyzes your face shape (round, oval, square, heart, or diamond) and recommends taper styles that complement your features. The tool even generates AI previews showing YOUR face with each recommended style.

How accurate is the AI recommendation?

Our AI has analyzed thousands of face shapes and taper styles using Google's Gemini 2.5 technology. It considers face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences to deliver highly accurate matches.

Is this tool really free?

Yes. The entire tool is completely free, including AI-generated photo previews. No credit card required, ever.

Do you store my photo?

No. Your photo is processed securely to analyze face shape and generate previews, then immediately deleted. We never store photos or sell your data.

Can I use this at any barbershop?

Absolutely. Each recommendation includes detailed barber instructions with specific measurements and techniques. You can show the AI-generated preview to any barber.

How long does the analysis take?

Most users get full results in about 30 seconds. Photo previews can take an extra 20-40 seconds while the AI renders YOUR face with each recommended style.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The tool is fully responsive and works on iOS and Android phones, tablets, and desktops. You can upload a fresh selfie or pick from your camera roll.

Is this only for men?

Yes — Taper Empire is a men's barbershop tool, so the recommendation engine and the photo analysis are tuned exclusively for male-presenting subjects.

What if I don't like the recommendations?

Tap the rating below your results and tell us what missed the mark — every reply tunes the next version of the tool. You can also restart and try a different photo or quiz answers.

Will my barber understand the instructions?

Yes. Each recommendation comes with technical barber language — guard sizes, taper start points, top length, and styling notes — that any professional barber can follow. Show them the AI preview and the instructions and you're set.
